conj:coord

This relation is used to link elements connected with a coordinating conjunction such as and and or. The conjunction itself is linked to the second element with a cc relation. It is parallel to conj relation in UD and in SUD for written language.

In case of multipe coordinating constructions, we analyse them in a chain in contrast to UD that analyses them in a bouquet. Thanks to this, the relation distance is shorter. We also believe it is a more accurate representation of the cognitive process of coordination.
