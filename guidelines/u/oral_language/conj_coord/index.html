conj:coord

This relation is used to link elements connected with a coordinating conjunction such as and and or. The conjunction itself is linked to the second element with a cc relation. It is analogous to conj relation in UD and SUD for written language.

French

Naija

In case of multiple coordinating constructions, we analyze them in a chain in contrast to UD, which analyzes them as a bouquet structure. This helps to reduce cumbersome long-distance relations. We also believe it to be a more accurate representation of the cognitive process of coordination.
